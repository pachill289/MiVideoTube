🚀 MiVideoTube: Watch Your Favorite YouTube Videos Ad-Free

🔭 Description: A site designed for you: Watch your favorite YouTube videos without ads, and add images or HTTP pages like Wikipedia! 😉

🌟 Version 1.0:

- No login needed.

- You can add videos by URL.

- You can add some copyright-free images and HTTP pages such as Wikipedia.

- You can delete the history of added videos.

- You can enable or disable the URL history.
